# 📖 Usage Guide

**How to use AI Vocab Builder Skill**

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Basic Usage

```bash
/english-vocabulary
```

The skill will guide you through:
1. **Learning Phase** - 35 vocabulary words
2. **Testing Phase** - 15 questions
3. **Results Phase** - Detailed feedback

---

## 📚 Understanding the Skill Flow

### Stage 1: Daily Learning (5-10 minutes)

**What you receive:**
- 35 new vocabulary words
- Pronunciation guide for each word
- Simple, clear meaning
- 3 contextual examples
- Usage frequency rating

**Example:**
```
Word 1: Abandon
Meaning: To leave something behind; to stop doing something.
Pronunciation: /əˈbændən/

Examples:
1. The ship was abandoned by the crew during the storm.
2. She abandoned her old hobby to focus on music.
3. Don't abandon your friends when they need help.

Frequency: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Common)
```

**What to do:**
- ✓ Read each word carefully
- ✓ Say pronunciation out loud
- ✓ Read all three examples
- ✓ Understand how word is used
- ✓ Take mental notes

---

### Stage 2: Daily Test (5-10 minutes)

**Test Structure: 15 Questions Total**

**Section A: Multiple Choice (5 questions)**
- Choose correct answer from 4 options
- Tests basic understanding

**Section B: Fill in the Blank (5 questions)**
- Complete sentence with correct word
- Tests recall and usage

**Section C: Sentence-Based (5 questions)**
- Read sentence with vocabulary word
- Answer what word means or how it's used
- Tests comprehension

**Example Test:**
```
SECTION A: MULTIPLE CHOICE

1. What does "abundant" mean?
   A) Very small
   B) Having plenty of something
   C) Hard to find
   D) Expensive

SECTION B: FILL IN THE BLANK

6. The garden has an _____ collection of flowers.

SECTION C: SENTENCE QUESTIONS

11. In this sentence: "The team showed resilient strength."
    What does "resilient" mean?
```

**Important:**
- ⏸ Take time to think
- 🤔 Use your best judgment
- 📝 Write/type your answers
- ⏱ No time limit (work at your pace)
- ✋ Answer all questions before seeing results

---

### Stage 3: Results & Feedback (2-3 minutes)

**You receive:**

1. **Answer Key**
   ```
   Question 1: Correct Answer: B | Your Answer: B ✓
   Question 2: Correct Answer: abundant | Your Answer: absent ✗
   ```

2. **Score Summary**
   ```
   Correct: 12 / 15
   Percentage: 80%
   Grade: GOOD
   ```

3. **Performance Breakdown**
   ```
   Words Mastered (12):
   ✓ abundant, beautiful, diligent, eloquent...

   Words to Practice (3):
   ✗ fragile, meager, gracious
   ```

4. **Performance Rating**
   - **Excellent** (90-100%): Great job! Strong vocabulary skills!
   - **Good** (70-89%): Good work! Keep practicing these words.
   - **Needs Practice** (Below 70%): Keep learning! Practice helps memory.

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: How often should I use the skill?

**A:** Daily is best for retention!
- 1x per day: Good
- 2x per day: Better (one new + one review)
- 3x+ per day: May cause burnout

### Q: Can I skip days?

**A:** Not recommended, but possible.
- 1 day skip: OK
- 2+ days: Review previous day first
- Best: Consistent daily practice

### Q: What if I get low score?

**A:** Normal! Here's what to do:
1. Don't get discouraged
2. Review words you missed
3. Create flashcards for difficult words
4. Practice using them in sentences
5. Take the test again next week

### Q: Can I change difficulty level?

**A:** Yes!
- Need easier? → Start with Basic level
- Too easy? → Try Medium or Matric
- Mix levels? → Ask Claude for custom words

### Q: How do I remember all 35 words?

**A:** Use multiple strategies:
1. **Spaced Repetition** - Review on days 1, 3, 7
2. **Context** - Use words in sentences
3. **Grouping** - Group similar words
4. **Visualization** - Draw pictures for words
5. **Pronunciation** - Say words out loud

### Q: Should I write down words?

**A:** Highly recommended!
- Writing helps memory
- Create vocabulary notebook
- Add your own examples
- Draw pictures/diagrams
- Review written notes regularly
